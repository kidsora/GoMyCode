Ojja

Ojja is a traditional Tunisian dish of eggs poached in a spicy tomato and harissa sauce, with garlic and peppers. Served with merguez sausage and fresh bread. Bold, flavorful, and satisfying.

  • Price : 8 D.T
Kafteji

Kafteji is a Tunisian vegetable medley of fried pumpkin, potatoes, peppers, and tomatoes, mixed with eggs and spices. Served chopped and blended for a flavorful, hearty dish. Perfect with fresh bread..

  • Price : 6 D.T
Plat Tunisien

Plat Tunisien: A classic Tunisian dish featuring fried egg, grilled peppers, potatoes, and fresh tomato, served with tuna, olives, and harissa. A simple yet flavorful combination, perfect with bread.

  • Price : 6 D.T

About Us

Welcome to our Tunisian restaurant, where we bring the vibrant flavors and rich culinary traditions of Tunisia to your table. Our menu is crafted with authentic, fresh ingredients, showcasing iconic dishes like Ojja, Kafteji, and Plat Tunisien, each bursting with bold spices and unique textures. From the savory warmth of harissa to the comforting flavors of slow-cooked stews and fresh vegetables, every meal is a journey through Tunisia's rich food culture. Whether you're here for a casual meal or a special occasion, we invite you to savor the tastes of our homeland with every bite.
